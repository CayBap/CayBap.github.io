Conference organizers

Prof. NGUYEN Van Noi

(HUS-VNU)

Conference Co-Chairs

Prof. NGUYEN Van Noi (HUS-VNU) Conference Co-Chairs He specializes in environmental chemistry, environmental science, and environmental remediation technology. Professor Noi's research interests include development of transition metal-based catalysis for the treatment of persistent organic pollutants in water and wastewater; synthesis of pillared clays for photooxidation processes and development of organoclays for sorption of organic pollutants. He is a rector of VNU University of Science, Vietnam National University and director of VNU Key Laboratory of Advanced Materials for Green Growth (KLAMAG).

Prof. AJIT Kumar Sharma

(Lovely Professional University, India)

Conference Co-Chairs

Professor Ajit Kumar Sharma is now a professor at Department of Chemistry, Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar, Punjab, India. He is also a member of International Association of Advanced Materials (IAAM) and Society of Environmental Chemistry and Allied Sciences (SECAS). His specialties are in environmental chemistry, nano-materials, advanced materials for environmental control.
